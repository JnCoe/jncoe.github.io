About Me

I am a data professional who believes that technology is only as good as the progress it fosters. With over 10 years of experience, I have navigated the rigorous demands of academia, led high-stakes investigations in the third sector, and am now driving innovation in the private industry. My diverse and multidisciplinary background allows me to act as a translator, bridging the gap between complex technical possibilities and the people they are meant to serve.

I thrive on envisioning the full lifecycle of a product, ensuring that every technical decision brings us closer to a tangible, human solution. This vision is grounded in a decade of deep technical fluency in Python, SQL and R used to build the underlying engines that turn abstract concepts into reliable, data-driven realities.
